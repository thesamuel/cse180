Asking Questions, Getting Assistance, Giving Feedback

It is very important to us that you succeed in CSE 180! Outside of lectures and sections, there are different ways to ask questions or discuss course issues:
  1. Submit a question using SkillItUp!
  2. Visit office hours (see Events above)! In addition, if you need extra time or need to discuss something in private, feel free to email and make an appointment.
  3. Send anonymous feedback to the course staff. The instructor will try to address the issue and share it with others only as appropriate, but will not have a way to reply to you without addressing the whole class.
